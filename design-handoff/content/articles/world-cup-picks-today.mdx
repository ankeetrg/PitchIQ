## Start With the Favorite, Then Test the Price

The best team is not always the best bet. If Brazil are -250, the question is not whether Brazil are better. The question is whether that line leaves any edge.

PitchIQ shows the pick as AI-labeled analysis. It is a projection, not certainty.

## Watch Line Movement

Late movement matters most when it lines up with news. A price shortening after confirmed lineups is different from a price shortening because the public likes a name.

If the move is already gone, do not chase it. Better prices return more often than impatient bettors think.

## Fantasy News Can Move Betting Markets

Lineups hit both markets. A star forward missing changes anytime scorer props, team totals, and fantasy ownership in the same window.

That is why PitchIQ keeps live scores, fantasy picks, and odds context on the same match page.

## Today's Rule

Back clear edges. Skip thin prices. If the line implies 59% and the model sees 60%, that is not enough.
