## France Have the Cleanest Ceiling

France can win matches in three ways: transition speed, set-piece size, and individual finishing. That is a brutal mix for group-stage opponents.

The risk is not talent. The risk is whether the midfield stays connected when the game gets stretched.

## Mbappe Changes the Math

Kylian Mbappe forces conservative defending before the first pass. Opponents shade cover to his side, which opens space for late runners and cutbacks.

That is why France props often move early. The market knows where the danger starts.

## The Fullbacks Are the Test

France can look unbeatable until elite wingers pin the fullbacks deep. That is the matchup to watch in knockout games.

If France keep the wide channels clean, the rest of the bracket has a problem.
