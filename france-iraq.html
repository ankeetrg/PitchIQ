France: France Turn Up the Volume

France at full strength against Iraq is one of the most lopsided Group I matchups on paper. Mbappé, Griezmann, and Dembélé against Iraq's 4-5-1 defensive block — the French forward line has been here before and knows how to break organized defenses.

Deschamps may give minutes to Thuram and Kolo Muani as the match progresses. Even France's squad depth — Marcus Thuram from Inter is a devastating option off the bench — overwhelms Iraq entirely.
